I work on visual SLAM, neural implicit 3D reconstruction, and place recognition for robots. My research includes the Vox-Fusion family of voxel-based neural reconstruction methods, feature-and-dense-matching SLAM, and benchmarking neural radiance fields for autonomous systems — bridging classical geometric vision with modern learned scene representations.
